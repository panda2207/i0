For Your Smile, Always

Many more happy returns of the day, my dearest one and my one and only bestest friend, Kiran. ❤️🎉

Kiran,

On your special day, I just want to tell you something that's always in my heart.

You may not think much about your health, but for me, your health is everything. Nothing in this world is more important to me than seeing you healthy, safe, and smiling. Your smile means so much to me, and seeing you smile makes my entire day more special. Your happiness brings peace to my heart, and that's all I ever want for you.

Sometimes I don't tell you these things, but every time I visit any temple, one of my prayers is always for you.

"Please always keep Kiran healthy, happy, and safe. Protect her from every illness, every pain, and every difficulty. Hold her hand through every step of her life and never let anything take away her beautiful smile."

Sai Baba, every single day I pray to You for Kiran with complete faith because I know You are always listening. I trust You with all my heart, and I know You will always take care of her.

Please bless her with a wonderful job very soon. She has worked so hard and waited patiently. She deserves every happiness and every success. Please open the right doors for her, remove every difficulty from her path, and bless her with a bright future.

One of my biggest wishes is to see the happiness on your face, Kiran, when you receive your first salary and buy beautiful gifts for your family. I know how much that moment means to you, and I pray with all my heart that Sai Baba makes that dream come true very soon and I pinky promise Sai Baba will do this all.

Thank You, Baba, for always watching over Kiran. Please continue to guide her, protect her, and shower Your endless blessings upon her. If I could ask You for only one thing, it would be to always keep her healthy. That alone is enough to make my heart peaceful.

I don't know how much I'll be able to do for you, but I promise I'll always do everything I can. No matter how hard life gets or how much effort it takes, I'll never stop trying to make your life a little brighter. Your happiness truly matters to me, and if I can be even one reason for your smile, that's enough to make my heart happy.

Kiran, I want you to know that I will always be excited to celebrate your success as if it were my own. Your achievements will always make me proud, and your happiness will always bring a smile to my face. No matter what challenges or problems come into your life, you will never have to face them alone. I will always stand beside you, support you, believe in you, and be there for you through every step of your journey. I will always pray for you, cheer for you, and celebrate every achievement you achieve because seeing you succeed is one of the greatest joys of my life.

Kiran, you are one of the greatest blessings in my life, and I thank Sai Baba every single day for bringing you into my life. No matter where life takes us, one thing will never change—I will always pray for you, always believe in you, and always be there for you.

Once again, many more happy returns of the day, my bestest friend. ❤️

May Sai Baba always hold your hand, bless you with good health, help you get the job you deserve, fulfill all your dreams, and keep that beautiful smile on your face forever.

Happy Birthday, Kiran. ❤️
